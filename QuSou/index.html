Abstract

刚柔耦合尾巴设计： 运用球状机构对机械结构设计中刚柔耦合尾巴部分进行设计，巧妙平衡结构刚度与通过性，满足在相关场景下拾取重物的功能需求。 腿足运动结构优化： 采用平移连杆机构对机器人运动结构展开设计，在确保整体负载合理的同时，提升机器人运动流畅性，增大其通过性与行动能力，使其可以更好地适应不同地形环境，灵活完成取物等任务动作。 建模与结构分析： 在整个设计过程中，借助 SolidWorks 专业软件进行精准建模与结构分析，同时结合 3D 打印技术制作实物模型，验证设计的可行性，并运用标准件设计技术确保各零部件的通用性与装配便捷性。 团队协作与项目管理： 统筹小组成员开展工作，合理安排任务分工，保障项目按计划有序推进。定期组织进行财务汇报，把控项目成本支出情况。全力辅助组长进行答辩准备工作，充分发挥沟通协调作用，确保整个项目顺利完成并取得优异成绩。

Rigid-flexible Coupling Tail Design: The rigid-flexible coupling tail part in the mechanical structure design was designed using a spherical mechanism, cleverly balancing the structural rigidity and accessibility to meet the functional requirements of picking up heavy objects in relevant scenarios. Optimization of Leg and Foot Motion Structure: The robot motion structure was designed using a translation linkage mechanism. While ensuring the overall load is reasonable, the robot's motion smoothness was improved, its accessibility and mobility were increased, so that it could better adapt to different terrain environments and flexibly completed tasks such as picking objects. Team organization and corporation: Coordinating team members to carry out tasks, assigning responsibilities appropriately, and ensuring the project progresses in an orderly manner according to the plan. Organizing regular financial reports to control project expenditure.
